§ 01 / Position

The argument I'm actually making.

Great PMM in B2B SaaS comes down to one thing: understanding the customer, sales motion, and product well enough to diagnose why revenue is stuck, then building the systems to unblock it.

I've spent 10+ years architecting marketing and sales systems for B2B SaaS. When a product isn't selling, the cause is usually structural: a misaligned pricing model or a broken sales motion.

I go into the field to find the buyer's true mental model, restructure the offering, and then use my automation background to build workflows that scale the new playbook. I run win/loss interviews AND wire Gong into NotebookLM to synthesise the data.

Strategy that unblocks revenue, powered by AI systems that remove the grunt work. That's the bet.
