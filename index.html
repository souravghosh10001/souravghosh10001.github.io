§ 01 / Position

The argument I'm actually making.

PMM in 2026 isn't about producing assets. It's about wiring assets into the revenue motion so the right thing shows up in the right place at the right moment, automatically.

I've been building marketing automation since 2016. I managed Salesforce Marketing Cloud, ran Oracle Eloqua, and built lead management systems from scratch at Infosys. The shift to AI-first is the exact same muscle applied to newer tools.

What makes the work different is that I don't stop at the deck. I build the workflow that delivers the deck into the SDR cadence. I write the positioning AND the n8n logic that routes it. I run win/loss interviews AND wire Gong into NotebookLM to synthesise the data.

Strategy and execution, in the same week, by the same person. That's the bet.
