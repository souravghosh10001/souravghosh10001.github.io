§ 01 / Position

The argument I'm actually making.

PMM isn't just about creating launch decks. It's about deeply understanding the customer, sales motion, and product to diagnose why revenue is stuck—and building the systems to unblock it.

I've spent 10+ years architecting marketing and sales systems. When a product isn't selling, the answer is rarely "better copy." It's often a misaligned pricing model or a broken sales motion.

I go into the field to find the buyer's true mental model, restructure the offering, and then use my automation background to build workflows that scale the new playbook. I run win/loss interviews AND wire Gong into NotebookLM to synthesise the data.

Strategy that unblocks revenue, powered by AI systems that remove the grunt work. That's the bet.
